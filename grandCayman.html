Grand Cayman

We didn't spend a lot of time on Grand Cayman Island but this beautiful island is a destination we would visit again. The island itself is fairly small and is popular among scuba divers for its coral reefs, turtles and shipwrecks. You can even try a turtle dish if you have time. We recommend renting a sea kayak and paddling Seven Mile Beach. The crystal clear water and warm sun makes for a relaxing few hours. Land the kayak along a remote patch of sandy beach and snorkel out to the coral reef for some up close views of the tropical life just under the surface.

Tiki Beach is a great place to grab a drink and lounge around in the bath tub waters of the Caribbean.
