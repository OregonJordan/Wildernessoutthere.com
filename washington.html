Washington

From kayaking and hiking to caving and camping, there's a plethora of adventure options for everyone here in the Evergreen State! The Pacific Northwest in my opinion has some of the most beautiful wilderness in the United States but with that beauty comes buckets of rain! The Cascade Mountain range serves as a giant playground separating a lush green coastal range from the barren dry eastern half of the state. In that green playground is where you will find us. You could spend a lifetime exploring Gifford Pinchot National Forest's 1.3 million acres which span from Mount Rainer to Mount Saint Helens to Mount Adams. Here you will find countless hidden waterfalls the likes of which rival that of Iceland's Skogafoss. Stand at the base of Falls Creek Falls 335 foot drop for a truly humbling experience. Jump in your 4X4 and head up an extremely rough forest road to Silver Star Mountain. Also located in Gifford Pinchot Forest this hike takes you up to a peak of 4,300ft. with fantastic panoramic views of Mount Rainer, Mount Saint Helens and Mount Adams.

Grab your head lamp and explore the hundreds of secret caves scattered all around the forest. Mount Saint Helens' volcanic activity has created a network of lava tubes that span for miles some of which you can hike through. The most well know of these lava caves is The Ape Caves. The main passage is 2.5 miles long with an entrance and exit at both ends. Although it is a popular destination for cavers it is not an easy scramble requiring some bouldering skills and the ability to scale an 8ft. vertical wall without assistance. You can always turn back at the wall!

 Trip Tips

  • Again, skip any tours
  • Pretty much everywhere has a parking fee
  • You will run into loose dogs on trail
  • Carry sunblock
  • Carry plenty of water
  • It's always smart to have some snacks
  • Throw a sleeping bag in your car
    during the colder seasons
  • Trekking poles help with elevation hikes
  • Plan for rain and dump what you don't need
  • Wetsuits (optional) but highly recommended
