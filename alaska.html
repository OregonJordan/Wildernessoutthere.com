Alaska

Ready for a 48 hour Alaska blitz? So are we! You can really do Alaska on the cheap! Flights to Anchorage round trip are cheaper than you might think. Booking accommodations outside of town will also bring down the cost. Rent a car and drive north 2 hours to Matanuska Glacier. Matanuska is the largest glacier accessible by car in the United States spanning 27 miles long by 4 miles wide which means, the journey is endless! You can bring your own crampons and take a nice easy walk out to the glacial falls less than a mile from the parking lot or you can pay to have a guide take you much further. We prefer further and it's hard to fly with an ice axe! We recommend Nova Alaska for your guide service. They offer a variety of tours based on your comfort level but we recommend you spend the extra few bucks for the "Adventure Trek". You'll spend all day out on the glacier and you'll go much further with an optional ice climbing section.

Once you're done with the glacier head south from Anchorage about an hour to Chugach State Park. There you can check out the Alaskan Wildlife Conservation Center. This place has an amazing display of animals ranging from red fox, moose to bears. Admission is cheap and there is even a restaurant on location. Since you're already there, head six more miles to Portage Glacier Cruises. There you can take a cheap boat ride around the lake to the base of Portage Glacier which has impressive views of glacier falls. Don't forget to check out the many other sights along the way!
