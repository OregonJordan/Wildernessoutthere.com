About Us!

Hello and thanks for visiting our adventure blog. This is a simple expression of us and our life. We're a simple kind of people that love to explore. We're most interested in getting away from the crowd and finding our own way. Over the years we've discovered some pretty cool places and so can you if you're willing to rough it a bit, ok maybe a lot in some cases! We're not glampers and we travel on the cheap so don't expect to find five star accommodations here or REI gear. Most of the gear we use is either used or cheap and for the most part; it all works just as well as the high end stuff.
